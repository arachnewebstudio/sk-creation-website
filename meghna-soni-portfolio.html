The Woman Behind the Mic

Where Stage
Meets Soul

I'm Meghna Soni — and the moment I pick up that mic, something magical happens. Not just for me, but for every single person in that room.

With over 8 years of experience commanding stages across India — from intimate candlelit weddings to high-octane corporate galas — I've come to believe that great anchoring is not about reading from a script. It's about reading the room, feeling the energy, and becoming the heartbeat of your event.

Fluent in Hindi & English. Warm, witty, and wonderfully present. Whether your evening calls for elegant grace or playful spark, I show up fully — so your guests leave with stories they'll tell for years.
